Unit 1: Domestic Storytelling

This unit explores the beauty and intimacy of everyday life at home. Through my lens, I captured moments of comfort, routine, and quiet emotion within familiar domestic spaces.

Stargazing Gaze

A close-up of a cat's eye reflecting stars, representing imagination and quiet wonder.

Composition: Extreme close-up focuses all attention on the eye, drawing viewers in intimately.

Lighting: Natural side lighting highlights the textures and depth of the fur and eye.

Element of Design: Texture—detailed fur and glistening eye surface stand out.

Principle of Design: Emphasis—everything directs the viewer to the sparkling eye.

Silent Footsteps

Fresh paw prints left on pristine snow suggest mystery and solitude.

Composition: Leading lines of the paw prints guide the eye naturally through the scene.

Lighting: Ambient daylight keeps the snow soft and untouched.

Element of Design: Pattern—repeating prints establish rhythm and presence.

Principle of Design: Rhythm—consistent spacing creates a flow across the image.

Quiet Study

A still life of books and a flower conveys a peaceful moment of reflection.

Composition: Rule of thirds balances the books and plant across the shelf.

Lighting: Soft natural light gently illuminates the wooden textures.

Element of Design: Shape—rectangular books vs. round petals contrast subtly.

Principle of Design: Balance—visual weight is evenly distributed.

Warm Nest

A cat snuggled on a blanket evokes coziness and contentment.

Composition: Framing using the blanket encircles the subject protectively.

Lighting: Diffused soft light enhances the warmth and softness of the scene.

Element of Design: Line—curves of the blanket guide the viewer.

Principle of Design: Harmony—the entire image feels cohesive and gentle.

Carefree Stretch

A playful pose showing vulnerability and freedom in a familiar space.

Composition: Diagonal line of the body adds energy and motion.

Lighting: Flash lighting freezes the motion and sharpens shadows.

Element of Design: Contrast—light body against dark blanket creates separation.

Principle of Design: Variety—textures, tones, and angles provide visual interest.

Playtime Charm

A scene of a cat with toys captures whimsy and youthful spirit.

Composition: Centered subject with balance on both sides of the image.

Lighting: Indoor artificial light enhances the cozy, playful mood.

Element of Design: Color—bright plush toys add a pop to the muted environment.

Principle of Design: Unity—each object contributes to a single theme.

Framed Curiosity

A cat gazes through a circular paper opening, as if peeking into another world.

Composition: Foreground framing with paper leads attention to the center.

Lighting: Spotlight draws focus sharply to the face and objects.

Element of Design: Space—the framing suggests depth and curiosity.

Principle of Design: Focus—the center becomes a visual destination.
