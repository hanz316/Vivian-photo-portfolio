Hello, I'm Vivian Li

I’m a high school student and passionate photographer who enjoys capturing emotions, textures, and small moments that tell big stories. Whether it’s a quiet corner at home, a glowing candle in the dark, or the expressive eyes of an animal — I believe every image has a story waiting to be told.

In this photography course, I learned how to frame subjects more intentionally, experiment with light and color, and create conceptual meaning in my work. Each assignment allowed me to grow in both technical skills and creative thinking.

My preferred style blends natural light, storytelling, and artistic editing. I’m inspired by everyday life, emotional visuals, and a bit of imagination.

In the future, I hope to continue using photography to express myself, document the world around me, and explore the power of visual storytelling.
